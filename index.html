About Us

Hi everyone, my name is George. It is with an absolute honor that I welcome you to my services. I am a 18 year old entrepreneur starting my dreams young. I know you may seem thrown off by my age but I can assure you that my work and attention for detail is just as good if not better than the surrounding detailing shops.
